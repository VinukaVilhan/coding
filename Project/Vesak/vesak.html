The Three Major Events Of Buddah's Life

Birth

Gautama Buddha, the founder of Buddhism, was born in Lumbini, Nepal, around the 6th century BCE. His birth is celebrated on Vesak Poya Day as a significant event in the Buddhist calendar.

Learn More
Enlightenment

Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi tree in Bodh Gaya, India, after years of meditation and ascetic practices. This pivotal moment marks the awakening to profound insight into the nature of existence.

Learn More
Death

Gautama Buddha passed away at the age of 80 in Kushinagar, India. His death, known as Parinirvana, marks his release from the cycle of birth and rebirth.
